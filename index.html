Welcome to the breakfast club

About us

So who are we? Well... we are a collection of people from all over the world dedicated to helping people get inspired to make the most important meal of the day, BREAKFAST!!!

Is that it? So apart from trying to encourage people to eat breakfast, our members take pride in what we make uploading their breakfast's to our social group chats with a brief description of the recipe and how long it took.

Not only do we do that selected posts get put on our Instagram and get pinned on our discord for easy access and use by our members and followers.

Our Mission

It's simple our mission is to grow a healthier world encouraging people to make breakfast.

We want to build a community of people eager to help others and show off their breakfast's.
